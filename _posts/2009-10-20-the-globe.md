Since it's my birthday, I'll share my new favorite drink:

The Globe from the (SFist)[http://sfist.com/2009/04/17/sfist_drinks_the_globe_cocktail.php].

* 3 oz. 209 Gin
* 1 oz. St. Germain
* 1 oz. lime juice 
* 1/2 oz. agave nectar

Shake and serve up with a mint garnish.
